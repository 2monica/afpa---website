About AFPA

AFPA is a youth-led initiative in Otjimbingwe dedicated to job creation, cultural preservation, and sustainable community development. We are turning heritage into opportunity by restoring the historic Powder Tower, building a cultural village, and creating a community development center.

Our Projects

  • Restoration of the 1870 Powder Tower as a tourist attraction
  • Construction of a Community Development Center on the historic Helbich Shop site
  • Creation of a Cultural Village showcasing traditional practices and crafts
  • Development of a youth-run restaurant and cultural events space
